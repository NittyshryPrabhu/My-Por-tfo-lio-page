About Me

Nittyshry Prabhu
Born on January 28, 2005, in Bhagalpur, Bihar, India. Currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML) from a college in Delhi affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. He identifies as a passionate writer, creative imagineer, and aspiring engineer, balancing analytical and literary pursuits.

His Writings & Creative Side-
Nittyshry is active in creative writing, authoring several blog posts and literary pieces, such as:
“Behind the Beautiful Lady” – an autobiographical piece highlighting his mother’s support and personal dreams and struggles.

Essays like “Undiscovered Me”, “Mitti Ki Khushboo”, and “The Process of Our Birth”, reflecting philosophical introspection, identity exploration, and emotional storytelling. His writing indicates thoughtful reflection on self‑discovery, family bonds, and creative expression.

Academic Focus & Aspirations-
Studied in his home village of Bhramarpur up through high school; then relocated to Delhi for his undergraduate studies. He shows a strong interest in technology (AI/ML), but is also deeply invested in creative and literary themes. His mother’s encouragement plays a central role in his ambition and resilience.

Nittyshry Prabhu has written two books so far.



1. A Diary [ A Lot Word ]
2. Slip of Mind's Pen
